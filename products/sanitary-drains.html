FoodSafe Drains Alliance Partner

Purpose-built drainage for food and beverage processing

SaniCrete is an official FoodSafe Drains Alliance Partner. That means when we install a floor, we can install the drainage system too — all from one source. The drains are leveled, tied into rebar for load rating, and finished with a seamless floor interface. No gaps, no joints, no places for bacteria to hide.

FoodSafe Drains are designed from the ground up for food and beverage facilities. These aren't generic off-the-shelf drains — they're NSF and USDA approved, built to food-safe design and fabrication principles per ANSI 3-A, and engineered to eliminate the bacteria harborage that makes outdated drain systems a contamination risk.

When drains and floors come from the same installation crew, everything fits. The slope is right, the interface is seamless, and the whole system works the way it should.

Why Drains Matter

They're a critical control point for food safety

Drains are one of the most overlooked parts of a food processing facility. When they're outdated or poorly installed, they become a source of standing water, bacterial growth, and contamination. Foodborne illness outbreaks have been traced back to failing drain systems.

FoodSafe Drains are built to address that. Fully welded stainless steel construction with no crevices or seams where bacteria can harbor. Multiple grate patterns for different flow requirements. Standard and custom sizes to fit any facility layout.

As an Alliance Partner, we handle the full scope — slope correction with our polymer concrete, drain installation, and the flooring system around it. One crew, one visit, everything integrated.

What We Handle

Drains and floors from the same crew

Drain Installation

We install FoodSafe Drains as part of our flooring projects — trench drains, area drains, and custom configurations. Leveled, tied into rebar, and finished with a seamless floor interface.

Slope Correction

We use our polymer concrete (SaniCrete PC) to re-pitch floors to drain properly before installing the drainage system. Eliminates standing water at the source.

Drain Replacement

Outdated or failing drains get cut out, replaced with FoodSafe Drains, and tied into a new flooring system. We handle the full demo and rebuild.

Complete Integration

The floor, the drains, the slope, and the cove base — all installed by one crew in one visit. Everything fits because everything comes from the same team.
