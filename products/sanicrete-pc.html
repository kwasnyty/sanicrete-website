Slope Correction, Drain Repair & Void Fill

SaniCrete PC (SaniBulk) is a high-strength polymer concrete engineered for slope correction, floor leveling, drain repairs, and filling voids in food processing facilities. When floors need to be re-pitched toward drains or standing water issues need to be resolved, SaniBulk delivers the solution with permanent results.

Whether you need to correct improper drainage pitch, rebuild deteriorated areas around floor drains, fill large voids, or build up transitions between areas, SaniBulk sets fast and bonds permanently to existing concrete substrates.
