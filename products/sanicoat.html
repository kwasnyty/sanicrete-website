SaniCoat

Epoxy Coating & Line Striping System

SaniCoat is our high-performance epoxy coating system designed for protective floor coatings and professional line striping. From safety markings and traffic lanes to full-floor protective coatings, SaniCoat delivers lasting color and durability in demanding environments.

Our line striping services are critical for food processing, warehouse, and distribution facilities where clearly marked pedestrian walkways, forklift traffic lanes, safety zones, and equipment boundaries keep operations running safely and efficiently.

  • Professional line striping & floor marking
  • Safety zone & traffic lane delineation
  • High-build epoxy coating
  • Chemical & abrasion resistant
  • Wide color selection
  • Fast return to service
  • OSHA-compliant safety markings
  • Forklift & heavy traffic rated

Line Striping Applications

Our professional line striping services include:

  • Pedestrian walkways & crosswalks
  • Forklift traffic lanes
  • Loading dock boundaries
  • Equipment placement zones
  • Safety & hazard zone markings
  • Aisle markings & directional arrows
  • Color-coded production zones
