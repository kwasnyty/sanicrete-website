No Obstacle Too Challenging: STX in Low Clearance Areas

Around the clock to finish before the washdown schedule

Home / Blog / Low Clearance Install
June 30, 2025 Project Spotlight

Not every flooring job is a wide-open production floor. Sometimes you're working under massive processing equipment with barely enough clearance to swing a trowel. That's exactly what this project looked like — and there wasn't an obstacle we couldn't handle.

The Challenge

Low clearance areas under heavy processing equipment. Tight spaces between conveyor legs, trench drains, and equipment pedestals. And a hard deadline — the floor had to be finished before the facility's next washdown schedule. Every minute counted.

The Approach

  • Around-the-clock installation — our crew worked continuously to meet the washdown deadline
  • SaniCrete STX — 3/8" stainless steel reinforced cementitious urethane in every accessible area
  • Fast-cure sealcoat — critical for this timeline, allowing the floor to be ready for washdown on schedule
  • Precision work around equipment — seamless transitions around pedestals, drains, and equipment legs

The Result

A seamless, sealed floor running under and around every piece of equipment in the area. The fast-cure topcoat meant the facility hit their washdown schedule with zero delays. Our crew doesn't back down from tight spaces or tight timelines — that's what we train for.

Products Used

  • SaniCrete STX — 3/8" stainless steel reinforced cementitious urethane
  • SaniCoat — Fast-cure sealcoat topcoat
