SaniCrete STX Installation at Food Processing Facility

The prep work and attention to detail shows in the final result

Home / Blog / STX Installation
September 8, 2025 Project Spotlight

We just wrapped up installing our 3/8" SaniCrete STX cementitious urethane flooring system at a longtime client's food processing facility. The prep work and attention to detail really shows in the final result.

Step 1: Surface Preparation

Every successful installation starts with proper surface prep. Our crew scarified the existing concrete to create the ideal surface profile for maximum adhesion. This step is critical — skip it or rush it, and the floor won't last. We take the time to get it right.

Step 2: SaniCrete STX Installation

With the substrate prepped and primed, our team poured and troweled 3/8" of SaniCrete STX across the floor. STX is our flagship stainless steel reinforced cementitious urethane — engineered for the toughest food processing environments. The crew works methodically, section by section, ensuring consistent thickness and a seamless finish throughout.

The Final Result

The finished floor is a seamless, vibrant, USDA-compliant surface ready to handle everything this facility throws at it — heavy equipment traffic, aggressive washdowns, thermal shock, and chemical exposure. From cold storage to production, this floor is built to perform for years.
