Flooring Solutions for Cold Storage & Distribution

Cold storage facilities experience extreme thermal cycling. Standard floors crack under this stress. SaniCrete systems flex with temperature changes without cracking.

Key Benefits

  • Thermal shock resistant (-40°F to 180°F)
  • No cracking from cycling
  • Forklift rated
  • Moisture tolerant
  • Fast cure in cold
  • Slip resistant

Typical Areas

Freezers, Coolers, Blast freezers, Loading docks, Distribution floors, Polished concrete for distribution centers
