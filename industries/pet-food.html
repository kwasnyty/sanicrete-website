Flooring for Pet Food & Animal Feed Facilities

Pet food manufacturing subjects floors to many of the same punishing conditions as human food processing — animal fats, rendering byproducts, hot washdowns, and heavy equipment traffic. The difference is the volumes are often higher and the raw ingredients more corrosive.

SaniCrete urethane concrete systems provide the seamless, chemical-resistant surfaces that pet food facilities need to maintain sanitary conditions and pass regulatory inspections.

Key Benefits

  • Animal fat and oil resistant
  • Thermal shock resistant
  • Heavy equipment and forklift rated
  • Seamless, joint-free
  • Easy to clean and sanitize
  • USDA / FDA compliant

Typical Areas

Rendering, Mixing and blending, Extrusion, Packaging, Raw material storage, Coolers, Washdown areas, Loading docks
