About SaniCrete

Family-owned and operated since 1990. Three decades of excellence in food processing flooring.

Home / About Us

One Throat to Choke™

That's not just a tagline—it's our business philosophy. SaniCrete is one of the few vertically integrated manufacturer and installer of resinous flooring systems in the food and beverage industry.

What does that mean for you? Complete accountability. We manufacture our own materials at our facility in Michigan, and we install them with our own trained crews nationwide. No subcontractors. No finger-pointing between material suppliers and installers when something goes wrong.

If there's ever an issue with your floor, you call us. We fix it. Period. That's the power of One Throat to Choke™.

Our single-source flooring solution involves manufacturing and supplying the specified flooring system materials, site evaluation, and project installation. We educate and train our own installation teams to ensure proper application, which we then stand behind with our long-term no-hassle warranty.

Our Leadership

Two generations committed to excellence in food processing flooring

Keith Kwasny

Founder & President

Keith started the company as a painting contractor in 1990. When a customer asked if he could do an epoxy garage floor, he said yes—and figured it out. Then came warehouses, then food processing plants. Each time someone asked, Keith said yes and delivered. That entrepreneurial grit built SaniCrete from the ground up. Over the past 10-15 years, the company developed its own proprietary flooring formulations, and today Keith continues to drive innovation and maintain the client relationships built over three decades of saying "yes, we can do that."

Tyler Kwasny

VP Operations

Tyler joined the family business starting as a laborer and working his way up through every position in the company over 15+ years. This ground-up experience gives him unique insight into operations, from mixing materials to managing complex installations. Today, he oversees all field operations, client relationships, and business development—bringing fresh perspective while maintaining the core values that built SaniCrete.

Our History

Three decades of innovation and growth

1990

Founded in Michigan

Keith Kwasny starts a painting and coatings company in Michigan. Early work in epoxy garage floors and warehouse coatings lays the foundation for what's to come.

1998

National Expansion

SaniCrete expands operations beyond the Midwest, taking on projects across the country.

2010

STX Innovation

Development of the patented SaniCrete STX system with helically twisted stainless steel wire reinforcement.

2015

Next Generation

Tyler Kwasny takes on leadership role after 10+ years working up from the field, bringing operational expertise to the executive team.

Today

Industry Leader

SaniCrete continues to grow, serving food processors nationwide with the same commitment to quality, innovation, and accountability that started it all.
