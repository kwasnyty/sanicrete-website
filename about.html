About SaniCrete

Family-owned and operated since 1990. Three decades of excellence in food processing flooring.

Home / About Us

One Throat to Choke™

That's not just a tagline—it's our business philosophy. SaniCrete is one of the few vertically integrated manufacturer and installer of resinous flooring systems in the food and beverage industry.

What does that mean for you? Complete accountability. Every product we install is our own proprietary formulation, and we install with our own trained crews nationwide. No subcontractors. No finger-pointing between material suppliers and installers when something goes wrong.

If there's ever an issue with your floor, you call us. We fix it. Period. That's the power of One Throat to Choke™.

Our single-source approach means we control the flooring system from formulation through installation — site evaluation, product specification, and project execution. We educate and train our own installation teams to ensure proper application, which we then stand behind with our long-term no-hassle warranty.

What We Do

Flooring for facilities where the floor actually matters

We formulate and install resinous flooring systems — cementitious urethane, epoxy, polymer concrete, and specialized coatings — for food and beverage processing facilities. Meat and poultry plants, dairy operations, bakeries, breweries, seafood processors, cold storage, pharmaceutical, and commercial kitchens.

Projects range from a small patch repair to a 5,600 SF full facility renovation done in a single weekend. We handle the full scope — surface preparation, slope correction, drain installation, cove base curbing, and the flooring system itself. We have a complete product line (STX, SL, HF, and more) because different environments need different systems. A kill floor has different demands than a chemical storage room or a loading dock.

Everything we install is designed to hold up under the conditions food processors deal with every day — thermal shock, chemical exposure, heavy impact, constant washdowns, and USDA sanitation requirements.

Our Leadership

Two generations committed to excellence in food processing flooring

Keith Kwasny

Founder & President

Keith started the company as a painting contractor in 1990. When a customer asked if he could do an epoxy garage floor, he said yes—and figured it out. Then came warehouses, then food processing plants. Each time someone asked, Keith said yes and delivered. That entrepreneurial grit built SaniCrete from the ground up. Over the past 10-15 years, the company developed its own proprietary flooring formulations, and today Keith continues to drive innovation and maintain the client relationships built over three decades of saying "yes, we can do that."

Tyler Kwasny

VP Operations

Tyler joined the family business starting as a laborer and working his way up through every position in the company over 15+ years. This ground-up experience gives him unique insight into operations, from mixing materials to managing complex installations. Today, he oversees all field operations, client relationships, and business development—bringing fresh perspective while maintaining the core values that built SaniCrete.

Who We Are

A small crew that's been doing this a long time

SaniCrete is a small, tight-knit operation. A lot of the guys on our crew have been working together for years. Everyone has done the work — mixing material, running scarifiers, troweling floors. Nobody here just manages from a desk.

We show up, protect your facility, do the job right, and clean up when we're done. That sounds simple, but it's why a lot of our clients call us back for a second project, then a third, then a fourth. We're not the biggest company in this space. We just do what we say we're going to do.

If you talk to our customers, the thing you'll hear most is that we're easy to work with and we don't leave problems behind. That matters more than anything on our website.
